Is the game worth it?

Star Citizen is currently a playable alpha. At its current state, you will be playing a space simulator with FPS elements. The game still lacks many features, has plenty bugs, and does not even have all of its gameplay loops (which are things like mining and trading) yet. The game currently only has one system with four planets. Each planet has their own satellites.


Despite being an incomplete game, it is a fun game to test out. If you are looking for a finished game, then do not spend money on this game. If you do enjoy it and do not mind all the setbacks of playing the game in its alpha state, then pledging for the game is a great deal.



Star Citizen is:

  •   • the most successful crowdfunding project ever
  •   • the most ambitious space simulator (and possibly even game) ever
  •   • an MMO, Space Sim, FPS, Mining, Racing, Flight Sim, and many more in one
